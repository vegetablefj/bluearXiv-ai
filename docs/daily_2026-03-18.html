math.AG

A Quadratically Enriched Count Of Lines In Smooth Del Pezzo Surfaces Of Degree 2 And 4
Victor Chachay
math.AG

math.AG math.NT

The cohomology of Hyperquot schemes on curves via shifted Yangians in type A

math-ph math.AG
