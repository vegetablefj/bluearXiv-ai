Solvable Automorphism Groups of Varieties ⭐ 精选
Serge Cantat et al.
math.AG math.GR
本文证明了拟仿射variety \(X\)的自同构群\(\mathrm{Aut}(X)\)中由包含恒等映射的不可约族生成的solvable子群必为algebraic subgroup。作为应用，对任意variety \(X\)，证明了\(\mathrm{Aut}(X)\)的每个连通solvable子群包含于某个Borel subgroup且其derived length \(\leq n+1\)，并定义了solvable radical和unipotent radical。特别地，若\(X\)是连通拟仿射variety且存在derived length为\(n+1\)的Borel subgroup \(\mathcal{B}\subset\mathrm{Aut}(X)\)，则\(X\)同构于仿射空间\(\mathbb{A}^n\)且\(\mathcal{B}\)共轭于Jonquières subgroup。该工作与关键词中的automorphism group和rationality of varieties高度契合。
