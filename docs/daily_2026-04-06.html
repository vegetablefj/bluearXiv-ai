Higher Chow cycles, cyclic cubic fourfolds and Lagrangian subvarieties
Shouhei Ma, Ken Sato
math.AG

Algebraization of absolute perfectoidization via section rings
Ryo Ishizuka, Shou Yoshikawa
math.AG math.AC math.NT

The rationality problem for multinorm one tori, II
Sumito Hasegawa, Kazuki Kanai, Yasuhiro Oki
math.AG math.RA
本文研究了与有限étale代数相关的multinorm one tori的稳定有理性与收缩有理性问题. 结果根据各分量次数的最大公约数\(d\)进行分类, 并给出了\(d>1\)时收缩有理性失效的充分条件.
Locally finite solvable Lie algebras of derivations
Mikhail Zaidenberg
math.AG
本文研究了仿射簇上由局部有限李代数生成的有限生成可解李代数的局部有限性问题. 作者给出了一些判定局部有限性的准则, 并在仿射平面这一特殊情形下给出了肯定的答案.
Automorphisms of the boundary complex of $\overline{\mathcal{M}}_{0, n}(\mathbb{P}^r, d)$
Arjun Joisha, Siddarth Kannan
math.AG math.CO
本文计算了Kontsevich模空间\(\overline{\mathcal{M}}_{0, n}(\mathbb{P}^r, d)\)中边界除子的对偶复形\(\mathsf{T}_{d, n}\)的自同构群。结果表明，当\(d \geq 2\)时，\(\mathrm{Aut}(\mathsf{T}_{d, n}) \cong \mathbb{S}_{n}\)，而当\(d=1\)时，\(\mathrm{Aut}(\mathsf{T}_{1, n}) \cong \mathbb{S}_{n + 1}\)。这一结果与Fulton-MacPherson紧化中边界复形的自同构性质相关联。
A semicontinuous relaxation of Saito's criterion and freeness as angular minimization

math.AG cs.LG math.CO

math.AG math.AC
