本文计算了Fermat曲面在数域上的Galois不变Néron-Severi秩, 给出了显式公式和表格, 但方法主要基于经典结果(如Shioda、Aoki) 和Galois模计算, 与您提供的关键词(如hyperkahler、K3 surface、period map等) 无直接关联.

\arxivwithtarget{2607.17459}{The deformed Vortex equations and equivariant stability conditions}{Tristan C. Collins, Yukai Zhang}\textbf{math.DG}, math.AG.

\arxivwithtarget{2607.17711}{Universal Correlators on Exponentially Ramified Spectral Curves}{Mohamad Alameddine, Alexander Hock}\textbf{math-ph}, hep-th, math.AG, math.CV.
