\arxivwithtarget{2604.24888}{The $\mathbf{P}^1$-motivic Gysin map}{Longke Tang}\textbf{math.AG}, math.AT, math.KT.

本文在$\mathbf{P}^1$-不稳定非$\mathbf{A}^1$-不变理论框架下, 为正则浸入构造了Gysin映射, 并统一给出了多种上同调理论中的Gysin映射构造.


\arxivwithtarget{2604.24903}{The Quasisymmetric Grassmannian}{Nantel Bergeron, Lucas Gagnon, Hunter Spink, Vasu Tewari}\textbf{math.AG}, math.CO.

本文构造了一个称为quasisymmetric Grassmannian的toric variety复形, 它是$\mathbb{C}^n$中$r$维平面Grassmannian的子簇, 其不可约分支为positroid variety. 该簇由Plücker坐标中形如$\Delta_A\Delta_{A'}=0$的方程定义, 并具有仿射铺砌结构, 其上同调环由拟对称多项式描述.


\arxivwithtarget{2604.25023}{The Mukai conjecture via Cox rings for special toric ambient embeddings}{Heath Pearson}\textbf{math.AG}.

本文研究了特征3下K3曲面上光滑有理曲线的3-可除$A_2^n$配置, 并完全分类了由此产生的三重覆盖.


\arxivwithtarget{2604.25238}{On the meagerness of the set of irregular bundles on Hopf surfaces}{Edoardo Ballico, Elizabeth Gasparim}\textbf{math.AG}.

本文证明了Hopf曲面上稳定丛模空间$\mathcal M_{r,c}$中由irregular bundles构成的子集是meager的, 即该子集在模空间中拓扑意义下非常稀疏.


\arxivwithtarget{2604.25265}{A local-global correspondence for perfectoid purity}{Ryo Ishizuka, Shou Yoshikawa}\textbf{math.AG}, math.AC, math.NT.

本文引入了(lim-)perfectoid splitting作为perfectoid purity的全局变体, 并建立了projective scheme的lim-perfectoid splitting与其Gorenstein section ring的lim-perfectoid purity之间的对应关系, 构造了新的lim-perfectoid纯环例子.


\arxivwithtarget{2604.25293}{Confocal families of plane algebraic curves}{Ragni Piene, Boris Shapiro}\textbf{math.AG}, math.CV.

本文研究了共享相同焦点集的平面代数曲线族, 通过焦点映射重新表述了共焦性, 并利用形变理论分析了其纤维结构.

\arxivwithtarget{2604.25637}{On the Jacobian algebras of Ziegler pairs of plane arrangements}{Alexandru Dimca, Piotr Pokora}\textbf{math.AG}.

\arxivwithtarget{2604.25340}{Level-rank dualities and moving vectors}{Wei Hu, Feiyue Huang, Yanbo Li, Xiangyu Qi}\textbf{math.RT}.

本文为所有经典affine Lie algebras统一构造了Fock spaces并建立了相应的组合模型(Maya diagrams和abaci) , 通过Virasoro algebra的作用刻画了Fock space中的joint highest weight vectors, 从而得到了level-rank duality理论. 该方法不依赖于character calculations, 并利用该理论解释了经典仿射型cyclotomic KLR algebra的defect与moving vector的关系.


\arxivwithtarget{2604.25462}{Representations of Super Yangians with Gelfand-Tsetlin bases}{Vyacheslav Futorny, Zheng Li, Jian Zhang}\textbf{math.RT}, math.QA.

\arxivwithtarget{2604.25511}{Atom spectra of symmetric monoidal abelian categories and classification of subcategories}{Shunya Saito}\textbf{math.RT}, math.CT, math.RA.

本文利用轨道原子谱(orbit atom spectrum) 作为商空间, 在满足张量理论假设的对称幺半Abel范畴中, 将子范畴的分类问题约化为Serre子范畴或torsion-free类, 并证明了与张量结构相容的torsion-free类可由轨道原子谱的任意子集分类. 该工作推广了交换Noether环上有限生成模范畴中的经典分类结果, 并应用于分次模、凝聚层及dg模等情形.


\arxivwithtarget{2604.25616}{Lie pairs and formal Lie groups}{Fulin Chen, Binyong Sun, Chuyun Wang}\textbf{math.RT}.
